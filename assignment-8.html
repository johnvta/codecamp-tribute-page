Kobe Bryant

One of the greatest basketball players of all time





Here's a time-line of Kobe Bryant's life:


  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

  • 1996 - Drafted by the Charolette Hornets and traded to Los Angeles Lakers

  • 1997 - Slam Dunk Contest Champion

  • 2000 - Beat the Indiana Pacers to win first NBA Championship

  • 2001 - Beat the Philadelphia 76ers to win second NBA Championship

  • 2002 - Beat the New Jersey Nets to win third NBA Championship

  • 2008 - Awarded the regular season's Most Valuable Player Award

  • 2009 - Beat the Orlando Magic to win fourth NBA Championship

  • 2010 - Beat the Boston Celtics to win fifth NBA Championship

  • 2012 - Won his final gold medal (won his first in 2008)

  • 2016 - Played his last season in the NBA (20 seasons)

  • 2018 - Won the Oscar for Best Animated Short Film

  • 2020 - Dies at the age of 41
